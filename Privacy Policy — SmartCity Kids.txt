SmartCity Kids is designed to help children and parents make safer everyday decisions.
We take privacy seriously and keep data usage to an absolute minimum.
📌 Data We Collect

Microphone input – used only when the user presses the “🎤 Speak” button.
No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted.
Voice processing happens locally on the device.

🚫 Data We Do NOT Collect

No personal identifiers
No names, emails, phone numbers
No location data
No accounts or logins
No advertising IDs
No analytics tied to a child

🧠 How Data Is Used

Voice input is converted to text locally.
The text is used to generate an on‑device response.
Nothing is sent to servers.

🗂 Data Storage

The app stores non‑personal settings only, such as:

earned badges
confidence values


This data is stored locally on the device using Android DataStore.

👨‍👩‍👧 Children’s Privacy

The app is designed for children.
No personal data is collected.
No data is shared with third parties.
Parental controls are provided.

🔐 Permissions

Microphone: Required only for voice interaction.
The app remains usable without granting microphone access.
